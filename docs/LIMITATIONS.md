# Current limitations

This prototype supports controlled fictional workflows and does not guarantee arbitrary-site compatibility. It is not ready for real sensitive accounts, real documents, OTPs, payments, financial transactions, medical-record changes, or government submissions. It does not replace a professional security, privacy, accessibility, or legal review and makes no worldwide novelty claim.

The public collaboration model is same-browser or demo-safe synchronisation without a production identity/backend layer. The extension is a local prototype restricted to controlled demo portals.
